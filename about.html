About Batson Lumber

Located in the heart of Batson, TX at 25860 Farm to Market Rd 770, Batson Lumber Company LLC has been a trusted provider of quality lumber products for both residential and commercial projects. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a cornerstone of the local construction industry.

With our extensive inventory and experienced team, we pride ourselves on offering premium wood products coupled with expert guidance to ensure your project's success. Whether you're a contractor, builder, or homeowner, we're here to serve all your lumber needs.
